Jordan 1's

The first shoe to ever be worn in the NBA with multiple colorways, the OG black and red was originally banned for violating the league’s uniform policy, and was threatened with a $5,000 fine every game it was worn.

The OG’s legacy lives on through the Air Jordan 1 Retro, honoring a legend that expanded the game’s creative potential and redefined basketball’s connection to style and culture.
